Projects

Pacemaker

January 2017

I was responsible for developing the user interface for the pacemaker. The interface can locally store 10 usernames/passwords, modify and save parameters for every patient, and transmit/receive information serially to the from the pacemaker board. The interface is also able to display the electrical activity of every chamber in the heart in real time.

Super Heterodyne Radio

March 2017

Developed operational familiarity with soldering irons and strong wiring skills by connecting and soldering electrical components to assemble a standard AM superheterodyne receiver.
